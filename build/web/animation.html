Media Graphics

B. Sc. in Media Graphics & Animation Degree Course





Earlier, Animation was not a very organized field and familiar only to a few. However, today with the technological development in this field and the increased applications and uses of Animation and Graphics in practically all spheres of life, everyone, including Govt. and Universities have taken a note of it and have introduced formal B. Sc. Degree Programmes, that focus on all that is to be learnt in this area.

B. Sc. Degree in Media Graphics & Animation is a specialized course of 3 years where a student is exposed to all facets of the industry. You will learn all the subjects which are relevant. Although you may specialize in one particular module, you will know all other aspects of Animation work cycle. In today's times of multitasking, companies prefer versatile candidates, who can, for example, be a modeler, a texture artist and can do visual effects also. One thus does not become redundant with a change in the work profile due to one's versatility.


ELIGIBILITY

Eligibility:

12th passed, any 3 years diploma recognized by board of technical education (Any state) or Dept. of Technical Education (DOT)

Documents Required:

  1. 10th STD. Mark Sheet and Passing Certificate
  2. 12th STD. Mark Sheet and Passing Certificate
  3. School Leaving Certificate
  4. 8 Photographs

Duration:

Three years consisting of six semesters


FIRST YEAR

Semester I & II

Introduction to Computers & Internet
Drawing and Sketching
Color Theory
Typography
Computer Graphics Part1: Adobe Photoshop
Computer Graphics Part2 : Adobe Illustrator
Technical and Creative Writing
Introduction to Multimedia and its Application
Developing Presentations
Design Principles
Print Media Part1 : Drawing
Print Media Part2 : Quark Express
SECOND YEAR

Semester III & IV

Introduction to Web Development
HTML
Computer Animation : Introduction to Flash
Content Digitization
Content Authoring on Web using Macromedia Dreamweaver
Developing Dynamic Web Pages using Java and VB Scripts
Video-Production Basics
Story Boarding
Visual Communication
Audio-Editing: Sound Forge
Video-Editing: Adobe Premier
THIRD YEAR

Semester V & VI

Animation Techniques and Types
Character and Background Design
Script Writing for Animation Films
Working on Flash
Introduction to 3D World
Project Work
