Bachelor in

Computer Applications

BCA Degree Course





Bachelor of Computer Application is a 3-year Degree programme making you a Professional Graduate in Software Application and Development. The students who want to pursue education in IT Software should be able to look at any problem analytically and should be interested in cracking the problem logically. This means the students should have basic inclination towards analytical and logical thinking and should be able to develop it further to be successful in this field.

A student wanting to make a career in IT with a focus on Software Development should do the Graduate Degree, BCA. This programme develops need based and job-oriented computer professionals to meet huge demand of IT skilled manpower in business and industries. The entire course is of 3 years duration, 6 semesters.

ELIGIBILITY

Eligibility:

12th passed, any 3 years diploma recognized by board of technical education (Any state) or Dept. of Technical Education (DOT)

Documents Required:

  1. 10th STD. Mark Sheet and Passing Certificate
  2. 12th STD. Mark Sheet and Passing Certificate
  3. School Leaving Certificate
  4. 8 Photographs

Duration:

Three years consisting of six semesters
